# Phase 4 — Product Branding

The name should evoke **authenticity, rhythm, behavioral cloning, or a digital twin** — and steer clear of "stealth/ghost/undetectable/bypass" language, which both misrepresents what the product can do (see the `isTrusted` reality in Phase 3) and signals the wrong use cases.

> ⚠️ **Avoid "TypingDNA."** It's the name of an existing keystroke-biometrics authentication company — a direct trademark and positioning conflict. The desktop folder is named `Typing_DNA`, but the product should ship under one of the names below.

## The shortlist

1. **Cadence** *(recommended)*
   *Your keyboard has a rhythm. Cadence learns it, so anything your assistant writes lands in your own hand.*
   — Clean, memorable, owns the "rhythm" concept; works as a verb ("Cadence it"). Note common-word trademark crowding; consider **Cadence Type** / **GetCadence** for the domain.

2. **Veritype**
   *Veritas meets type: a behavioral profile that keeps your writing unmistakably, authentically yours.*
   — Leans into authenticity/truth; distinctive and brandable.

3. **Keyprint**
   *Like a fingerprint, but for your keystrokes — a portable signature of how you type.*
   — Strong biometric/identity metaphor, instantly intuitive.

4. **Twindex** *(twin + index)*
   *Your typing digital twin, indexed into a profile your tools can carry anywhere.*
   — Hits the "digital twin" brief directly; coined and ownable.

5. **Tempo**
   *Set the tempo once; everything you send moves to your natural beat.*
   — Short, friendly, musical; pairs well with Cadence if you want a family.

6. **Echotype**
   *It echoes the way you actually type — pace, pauses, and all.*
   — Evokes faithful reproduction; modern and pronounceable.

7. **Penmark**
   *Digital penmanship: your personal mark on every keystroke.*
   — Bridges handwriting craft and typing; warm, human, premium.

## Recommendation

Lead with **Cadence** for consumer warmth and the rhythm story, with **Veritype** as the strongest backup if you want a coined, trademark-clean name. Pre-clear whichever you pick: USPTO/EUIPO search, `.com` (or a clean `.app`/`.io`), and the social handles, before committing.
