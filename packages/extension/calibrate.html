Cadence The Tuning Room

We're tuning an instrument that is you. Type naturally — there's no score.

You'll do a few short typing prompts (about 3 minutes total). As you type, you'll see your rhythm drawn live. At the end we build your typing profile and save it to this extension, so it types in your cadence.

  • Type the way you normally would — mistakes and all.
  • Don't try to be fast or perfect. We're measuring you, not testing you.
  • Nothing leaves your machine. The profile is stored locally.
